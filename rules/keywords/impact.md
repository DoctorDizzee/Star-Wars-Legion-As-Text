# Keyword: Impact X
**Type:** Weapon Keyword
**Timing:** During Attack: Offense
**Value Required:** Yes (X is a variable number)

### Official Rules Text
While attacking a unit that has Armor, change up to X Hit results to Critical results.

### AI Processing Logic
* **Condition 1:** Target unit must have the "Armor" keyword.
* **Effect:** Convert standard Hits to Crits, up to a maximum of the X value.
* **Interaction Note:** This happens *before* the defender applies the Armor keyword (which cancels standard hits).
